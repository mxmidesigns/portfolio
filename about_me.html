I gathered the courage and started a Bootcamp at CareerFoundry and realized generating creative ideas from user's point of view to create life-impactful design and beautigul experiences is what I enjoy doing. I love to understand and learn more about user behavior. I want to make sure people have a delightful experience with thoughful design.
