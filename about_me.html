Greetings! My name is Mai Nguyen. My regular days consists of mapping users' experience thanks to the methods of Design Thinking. I enjoy it a lot, even outside the digital world. I like to analyze interactivity in everyday life and explore concepts of aesthetics and interactive design. My goal is to make sure people have a delightful experience with thoughful design.

Before, I graduated from San Jose State Universiy with a Bachelor's Degree in Business. Many people wonder "What made you change your study?" Working 2 years at Apigee Corp. & Peloton Technology sparked up my interest into tech. I gathered the courage and started a Bootcamp at CareerFoundry and realized generating creative ideas from user's point of view to create life-impactful design and beautigul experiences is what I enjoy doing.

My interests include: Playing jazz music on the piano, reading on Cultural Anthropology, drawing with vector graphics, learning programming languages on the side, finding brunch places that sell avocado toast.
